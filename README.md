# Consumer Brand Performance Analysis

## Objective

Analyze consumer product sales data across multiple products and international markets to identify revenue drivers, market opportunities, and strategic recommendations for brand growth.

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Data Analysis
* Business Analytics

## Business Questions

* Which products generate the most revenue?
* Which markets perform best?
* What product categories drive growth?
* What opportunities exist to improve performance?

## Key Findings

### Top Performing Products

1. Tea Tree Moisturizer ($260,905)
2. Hydrating Face Serum ($250,323)
3. Hair Repair Oil ($232,865)
4. Anti-Aging Serum ($232,248)
5. Body Butter Cream ($222,924)

### Top Markets

1. USA ($628,488)
2. New Zealand ($557,060)
3. Australia ($505,498)

### Lowest Revenue Market

India ($346,435)

## Strategic Recommendations

* Increase investment in hydration and repair-focused product categories.
* Utilize the U.S. market as a testing ground for future product launches.
* Investigate growth opportunities in India through additional market research and customer analysis.
